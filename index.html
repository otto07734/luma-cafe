Our Story

A Gathering Place

Luma is more than coffee—it's a space where light meets community. We believe in the ritual of slowing down, in conversations that matter, and in crafting each cup with intention.

Nestled in the heart of Phoenix, we source beans from ethical roasters and serve them alongside locally-made pastries. Every detail is considered, every guest welcomed.
